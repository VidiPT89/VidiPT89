# Hi, I'm David! 👋

I started my journey in tech with a professional qualification in IT Equipment Management at Escola Digital, where I built a solid foundation in hardware, systems, and IT infrastructure. After that, I spent 16 years working professionally in photography, following a Bachelor's degree in Photography and Visual Culture (Cinema) at IADE — a career that sharpened my eye for detail, visual communication, and delivering quality work under pressure.

Looking for a new challenge, I made a deliberate move into software development by completing the Reskilling 4 Employment – Software Developer programme at CESAE Digital, a 1050-hour intensive course covering the full stack: SQL and NoSQL databases, object-oriented development, front-end and back-end web development, front-end and back-end frameworks, mobile development for Android and iOS, Quality Assurance, Design Patterns, UML, and project management in both Waterfall and Agile methodologies — including 400 hours of hands-on work experience.
