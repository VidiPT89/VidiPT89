## Who am I?

I started in tech with a qualification in **IT Equipment Management** -- hardware, systems, infrastructure -- before spending 16 years as a professional photographer.

That career gave me something most developers don't have: a trained eye for composition, an obsession with getting details right, and the discipline to deliver quality work under pressure.

When I decided to make the jump into software, I went all-in.

I completed the **Reskilling 4 Employment -- Software Developer** programme at **CESAE Digital**: 1050 hours covering the full stack, from databases and OOP to front-end, back-end, mobile, QA, Design Patterns, UML, and Agile -- plus 400 hours of real hands-on work experience.

I bring a creative mindset to technical problems. I care about how things look AND how they work.
